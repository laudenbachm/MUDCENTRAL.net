Module 4 : Items

Adamantite Chainmail

This heavy suit of chainmail armour gleams with the smooth darkness of the metal known as adamantite. Nearly indestructible and quite rare, only master smiths have the necessary skill to craft such works of art.

Bardic Lute

This rare lute is a remnant of the days of the legendary bards. Its resonance is perfect, its contruction a mastery of woodcraft.

Black Steel Halberd

This massive, unwieldly weapon has a wickedly-curved head, and is fashioned from a gleaming steel the colour of soot. Wound about its haft is a thin cord which provides a good grip.

Climbing Harness

This heavy leather harness is complete with hooks and rope. It is used by climbers to anchor themselves while climbing, and is invaluable when making the treacherous climbs up mountains.

Copper Ore

This rough chunk of rock contains a large portion of copper. Somewhat valuable, copper is used in many everyday applications. Before the copper can be extracted from the ore, it must be smelted and the rock skimmed off the surface of the metal.

Dwarven Axe

This heavy, one-handed axe is short-hafted and has a single blade shaped like a half moon. Well-balanced and durable, it is a fine example of the fact that daily dwarven handiwork greatly surpasses most works of masters of other races.

Dwarven Chainmail

This heavy suit of chainmail is a product of the masterful skill of the dwarves. Made of two thin layers of tiny, but unbelievably strong rings, it provides a level of protection seldom seen in light armours.

Dwarven Craft-Hammer

This heavy, two-handed hammer is imbued with powerful striking magics. It is used by the elite dwarven smiths to craft the most precious of metals-- mithril. These hammers are the only things that have the power to craft mithril, and consequently make incredible weapons in battle. However, they are lacking greatly in accuracy.

Dwarven Hammer

This light hammer has a rectangular head of the finest dwarf-iron. It is easy to swing yet hits hard, and would make an excellent weapon.

Room Ticket

This small ticket is good for a night's rest in an inn or tavern. Usually worth the price, as resting in a comfortable inn bed is more beneficial than anywhere else.

Fine Chainmail Tunic

This exquisite chainmail shirt is crafted from the finest high-steel. Painstaking work has gone into its shining interlinked rings. It would provide good protection from blows in combat.

Fine Greatsword

This heavy, two-handed sword has an extremely sharp four- foot blade that can punch through even the heaviest armour with ease. It is made from high steel and is commonly seen in the possession of high ranking officers.

Fine Mace

This sturdy weapon is crafted out of high-steel, and has been fashioned in such a way that is is excellently balanced. This allows for heavier hitting with no reduction in speed.

Fine Throwing Knife

This well-balanced, razor-sharp blade makes a formidable weapon at short range. It is both accurate and damaging in the hands of an expert.

Gnomish Fish-Helm

This strangely-crafted device is made to ressemble a fish's head. Covering the mouth and nose is an airtight wooden device that hooks up to a small pack of sort on the back. It allows breathing of water, but only under extremely cold conditions. Somewhat fragile, it is not useful in combat situations.

Golden Harp

This exquisite golden harp is crafted with intricate swirling patterns. Its strings are fine and well-strung, and the melody that it plays when its strings are plucked is heavenly.

Iron Crown

This light circlet is surprisingly finely-crafted, despite the fact it is only iron. An eye-sized amber stone set in its front glitters brightly. *Same AC as a Hard Leather Helm but -40 enc.

Iron Ore

This rough chunk of rock contains a large portion of iron. Useful in creating weaponry and eveyrday items, it can be refined to make steel. Before being usable, however, the iron must be extracted from the ore by smelting it.

Mine Pass

This small wooden card is etched with the symbol of the Miner's Guild of Khazarad. Only people bearing this pass may enter the mines.

Moonstone

This small, semi-precious stone is semi-opaque, and is white with a pale blue hue.

Ogre-Skin Shirt

This heavy shirt is made from the thick, boiled skin of an ogre. Known for their toughness, Ogres are usually unwilling to part with their skin, but when the occasion arises it makes excellent armour.

Pickaxe

This large tool has a heavy metal head, one side of which has a curved spike, the other sporting a broad axe-like blade. It is indispensible to miners, who use it to hack out the rock and then to break it up to expose the ore. It also makes a good weapon.

Piece Of Jade

This small, fancy stone is a milky green in colour.

Runic Warhammer

This massive, heavy hammer's granite head is intricately carved with jagged runes of a crude sort. The runes glow a fierce red and seem to pulsate to your heartbeat. Its haft is of leather-bound ironwood, and appears well-worn.

Silver Flute

This beautiful silver flute sings with a clear, beautiful tone when played.

Silver Ore

This rough chunk of rock contains a large portion of silver. Fairly valuable, it is mainly used in jewlery and high- quality dishware. The ore must be smelted so that the silver can be extracted-- until then, it is nearly worthless.

Swamp Boots

This is a pair of rigid leather boots, reinforced with iron caps on the toes. They are solidly made, and provide good protection in combat.

Topaz Stone

This small, precious stone is a golden yellow in colour.
