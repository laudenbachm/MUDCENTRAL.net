***Kill the Ancient Gypsy Woman, return long golden chain to Enigma Lord***

The wretched woman finally is struck down, a screeching wail escaping her cracked lips, Around her waist you see the chain you have been told to seek out, it glows a mixture of red and yellow. You gingerly remove it from the waist of the hag and wonder if you almost heard a breath escaping from her ancient lungs. An ancient hand snakes out, grabbing hard into your wrist, you suddenly find yourself next to the woman's face and her voice sears into your mind, "I foresaw your visit here, and so I also foresee the mystic is against you, he is out to destroy you. With that her other hand slips a small vial into yours, as your hand encloses it your palm seems to be set ablaze for a moment. With that the woman is back on the floor, and you can't hear any breath from her lungs.
