Darkbane Quest
To complete this quest, you must be one of the following:
Level 50+ Good Aligned Paladin
Level 50+ Good Aligned Warrior
Level 50+ Good Aligned Missionary

The quest begins at the White Forest, Enterance
Read the Golden Plaque
Answer Riddle #1 (Riddle answers are at the bottom of this page)
Anyone who is not good alignment will be ejected from the forest.
Work your way through the white forest
(NW, N, N, N, E, NE, N, NE, Go Path, NW, NE, SE, S,
NE, SE, S, SW, E, Go Path, E, N, N, NE, NE, N, SW)
Read the Second Plaque
Answer Riddle #2
Follow the Path
(NW, NW, NE, NE, SE, SE, SW, W)
Read the Third Plaque
Answer Riddle #3
Go Northeast
Read the Fourth Plaque
Answer Riddle #4
Go West and meet Justice Darkbane

Greet Justice Darkbane
Ask Justice Darkbane Wretched One
Ask Justice Darkbane Holy Sword (You are given a key)
Ask Justice Darkbane Arlysia
(You are teleported to the western side of the city, by the docks)

Now find and kill the Death Minion.
Read Darkbane's words to find the minion in the new graveyard.
Use the platinum key he gave you to open the door.
You may want to open the coffin to face the Platinum Knight
The knight drops the spiked silvery armour.

Take the signet ring which is dropped back to Darkbane
Ask Justice Darkbane return
Ask Justice Darkbane Arlysia

You have completed the Darkbane quest, you will get 0 exp and a weapon reward! (The first player receives the Darkbane sword. The second player to complete the quest receives a Jewel Encrusted Warhammer and possibly a morningstar ring.)

Riddle Answers:

Riddle #1: Say Trees
Riddle #2: Say Time
Riddle #3: Say Love
Riddle #4: Say Courage
