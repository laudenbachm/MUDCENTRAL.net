NEUTRAL QUEST 4(level 40 restricted)
Items required to complete this quest are:

a flotation device (skiff, silverbark canoe, log raft)
golden idol
magma amulet (can be gotten later on from behemoth hellhound hides if needed)
1 runic
king crab claw
severed dragon claw
red rock key
polished key
feathered key

Goto Grey Lord
Ask grey missions

Go through the waterfall on the western end of the Silver River
kill king crab
dig with the claw

Goto Seher'Sahham
Ask Seher'Sahham fee (make sure you have 1 runic)

Go through the water portal
pick up a golden idol in lagoon

Go through the roof in NW corner of Lost City
Goto Gaunt One Messenger
greet messenger

Goto Gaunt One Elder
greet elder
ask elder agreed

Goto Aged Earth Dragon
kill him

Goto Gaunt One Elder
ask elder claw

Goto volcano
kill red demonling
kill lesser efreeti
kill dark phoenix

Goto Grey Lord
Ask grey destroyed

Quest Complete, you will get a cloak and 30 million Exp
