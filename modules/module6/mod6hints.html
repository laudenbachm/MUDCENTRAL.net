Module 6 - The Sands Of Time - Help/Hints

  1. Before entering the Scorching Desert, make sure you have at least one full waterskin.
  2. More for the Desert, watch for sandstorms.  The longer you stay in the desert the more likely you'll get caught up in one and brought into a different room.
  3. Women and Men at the Oasis are rakshashas, and the fruit they try to give you will poison you if you eat it. So don't. Poison is bad.
  4. Don't stay under water in the Shimmering Pool very long, you can suffocate. To get into the Pool, type swim pool.
  5. Pull the winch in the guardroom in the 2nd level of the Saracen Hideout to open the drawbridge.
  6. From the door with the metal plate, search north to get to the Saracen Commander.
  7. Once you start fighting zealouts, remember that you can walk through walls to find the Saracen High Priest.
  8. The Basalt Obelisk is a sign that tells you where stuff is, like the sign in Town Square in Silvermere.  Use the blue book in the library to translate it.  Look at the Mod 6 Quest walkthru to find more info on the library.
