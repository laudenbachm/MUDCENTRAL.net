Junkyard, Entrance
This dirty, rectangular plot is filled with all manner of heavy, useless objects, such as smashed statues, ruined carts, mangled furniture, and corroded armour. In the centre of the lot stands a strange, gleaming metal contraption, emblazoned with the High Guild of Magery's seal. A plaque on the opposite side to the seal apparently details instructions on its use. The junkyard continues to the east, and exits onto Temple street to the south.
Obvious exits: south, east
