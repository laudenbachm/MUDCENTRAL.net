New/Altered spells in module 1/mmud 1.1f

Mage-2 classes now get the L30 Dragonfire spell which has been reduced from 24 to 20 mana

vampiric touch: (vamp)
Now does 6-20 instead of 6-16

deathtouch: (dtch)

Now takes 24 mana instead of 12. It used to hit like sunbolt, and if you had just typed it by itself you'd attack yourself. Usually it was never used since sunbolt did the same and dtch you had to type almost exactly in order to cast. Well, it's been changed. It is now a very strong vamp touch.

Example:
You reach out and touch Minotaur!
Dark energy drains Minotaur of life for 54 damage!
You now have 54 more health! Who needs major healing now?

But, vamp touch and dtch now don't affect the undead now as well. Meaning stone golems, skeletal warriors, gelatinous cubes, etc, are now immune. Which makes the a/c a lot harder to try and solo. But, the dtch improvment makes up for it in other areas.

Rapid Healing: (rhel)

This used to be the rgen spell, and now is called rapid healing instead of regeneration. Don't know why it was changed, but works great still.

Regeneration: (rgen)

HP tick up! up! up! for about 30 seconds..


certain monsters drop spells now...

Dark Mages drop: grey rolled parchment
Spell: Frenzy
Level: 15 *Mage-2*
Mana: 18
This thin piece of parchment is covered in spidery letters. After scanning the document, you discover it is instructions on the learning and casting of a spell named Frenzy! The effects of Frenzy are to increase combat accuracy, damage, and speed, while reducing all defenses.

***this spell gets my seal of approval..***
Cast this on someone else and that person's average damage per round will SKYROCKET..!

Dark Wizard drops: tattered scroll
Spell: Mass Frenzy
Level: 20 *Mage-3*
Mana: 45
This long, ripped scroll is covered by row upon row of thin, evenly-spaced handwriting, describing the methods of learning and casting a spell named Mass Frenzy! This spell duplicates the effects of the Frenzy spell, but is cast upon oneself and one's allies.

Dark Priests drop: holy writ
Spell: Righteousness
Level: 15 *Priest-3*
Mana: 24
This thin manual describes the ways of properly praying to one's deity in order to be filled with righteous power! This Righteousness gives the target an increase in combat power, coupled with a small healing of one's wounds over time.

Dark Druids drop: barkskin scroll
Spell: Regeneration
Level: 15 *Druid-3*
Mana: 12
This thick, weathered bark scroll is inscribed with many passages written in a bold, flowing hand. Upon scanning its contents, you discover that it contains the secret methods of learning and casting the Regeneration spell! This powerful, but short-lived spell rapidly heals the target's wounds.
