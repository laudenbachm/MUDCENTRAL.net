Jael's Missile Weapons

This small, but well-kept shop has that newly-opened look to it, with everything in order and in pristine condition. Ranged weapons of all descriptions hang from the walls or are displayed in cases, and a carefully-scripted sign on the wall lists the wares available. The only visible exit is through the entrance to the west.

Also here: Jael.

Obvious exits: west

Jael smiles broadly as you approach, and bows slightly. He makes a wide sweeping gesture to his wares, and says, "Welcome, friend, to the finest bow shop in all of Silvermere! In fact, the only one!" He grins roguishly, and continues, "Please peruse my fine weapons, you will find nothing but the finest quality here. And at such bargain prices!"
