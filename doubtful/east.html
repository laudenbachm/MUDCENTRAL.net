Key to Pyramid 
1: Only have about 2 minutes to be on this level before dropping to scorched caverns so you better
   be moving like yer butt's on fire. Go to the * and push block to open the secret psg's. Certain 
   * opens the next psg while others opens the previous. 
2: This one is pure hell. I suggest run right thru it without wasting any time. Can't rest, sneak,
   nor hide and its so pitch black you'd think the sun no longer exists. Poisonous traps also come 
   at you from everywhere. 
3: This is just a headache. Nonstop doors opening and locking. Some doors will never open while 
   others do. Certain doors can't be opened but will open on their own. You'll bash so much that 
   you'll see stars. But act quickly, they don't stay open long. Find the Floating key and kill 
   it. Pick up the lion key (very quickly) before it poofs. Use it on the correct door to get to 
   the sphinx. 
4: It's simple if you think about it - only took me 14 tries. :) Solve the riddle then make moves 
   to spell out the answer.  To spell out the answer simply look at the plaques to determine the 
   letters for the exits. The perimeter-room letters are for the exit towards the inside. The 
   inner rooms hold 4 letters - each correspond to the exit in that direction of which it appears. 
   You also don't have to neccesarily hit all the letters with each move. It only counts when you 
   pass thru an exit with a letter - outside rooms don't count. 
5: Not much of a trick here. Just plain E-Z. 
6: You made it to the Pharoah! Hope you brought your red-iron weapons. :) If you log off you'll 
   end up back on level 5. Good luck!
