The Minotaur Champion

From the Labyrinth entrance (exits S,E,W), go:

W,S,S,S,S,W,W,W,N,N,W(through door),W,N,E,N,W,N(through door)N,E

You will be in a LABYRINTH PASSAGE (exits N,W) type "SAY RIVER". This is the answer to the first riddle and will open up a passage to the east.
Once the passage is opened, go:

E,N,E,E,N,N,W,S,W,W,N,N,W,N,W,S,W,N,N,E,E,E,S,E,E,S,W,S

You will be at a dead end (exit N). This is the first puzzle, to solve it:

PUSH MOON, TURN SUN RIGHT, PUSH MOON, PUSH STAR.

The puzzle must be completed in that order for the passage to open. Now go:

N,E,N (secret passage going north should now appear), go:

N,E,S,S,E,N,E,N,N,W,W,W,N,E,N,N,E,E,E,S,E,N,E,S,E,S

You will be at LABYRINTH PASSAGE (exits N,S) type "SAY HEARTBEAT". This is the answer to the second riddle and will open up a passage to the west.
Once the passage is opened, go:

W,W,W,S,E,S,W,S,E,E,S

You will be at LABYRINTH PASSAGE (exits N,S). SEARCH WEST until you find an exit to the west, then go:

W,W

You will be at LABYRINTH PASSAGE (exits E,W) in the room with a MINOTAUR STATUE. This is the second puzzle:

PULL RIGHT HORN, PUSH RIGHT EYE, PULL LEFT HORN, PULL NOSE RING, PUSH RIGHT EYE, PULL LEFT HORN, PULL NOSE RING.

This will open a passage to the south (note: other combos may work for this puzzle, but you might end up warped to the FOUL PIT)

The following passage (from MINOTAUR STATUE) will be pitch dark, you wont be able to see anything:

S,S,S,S,W,N,W,N,N,E,S,U.

You will be in a crystalline tunnel. Follow this passage (doesn't branch off) till you reach a room with exits S,W,E (closed door).

To the south, will be the MINOTAUR CHAMPION, (drops $$$, ruby key for chest, and sometimes bone-handled headcutter).

Use the ruby key to open the door and kill the chest, which drops any of the following: $$$, Golden broadsword, golden breastplate, rosewood staff, shimmering white robes, bone-handled headcutter.

To exit, back in the room with exits S,W,E(door), PULL LEVER

You will be in a FOUL PIT with no exits.

Type GO HOLE, and follow the watery tunnel for 12 rooms, which ends up back on the beach in front of the labyrinth.

Note: This is the only possible route to the Minotaur Champion. Any wrong turns and you'll find yourself teleported to some other part of the labyrinth, so take your time.
