Questers and Quest Spirits

The mystic spirit greets you with a bow, saying "Welcome young one. Thy travels have brought thee far, to find me in this cursed tomb where a am bound. The dark one was cruel indeed to keep my spirit in this plane after we were vanquished, but even in defeat there can be hope. I have little left to offer, save my experiences fighting the unnamed one. My skill is thine to learn from, and I would gladly train thee to the next level of advancement. One more thing can I offer you: seek the old one in the heart of the darkenwood, he alone lived through the Great Wars, and he alone has mastery enough to provide thee with the necessary learning you shall surely need in the future."

young girl

This dark-haired girl is clad in the white embroidered robes of a temple priest, though she looks far too young to hold such a position. Her wide blue eyes seem to hold much wisdom in them, and more than a little secrecy.

The young girl smiles at you, saying "Thank you, brave one. I would surely have been slain had you not arrived in time. You have earned the right to proceed further. Turn the right candle to your left, and the path shall be revealed to you."

mystic spirit

This is image of a well-built half-orc, wearing nothing but a loose pair of white pants. He stands in such a manner as to suggest that if you blinked your eyes, he could be upon you before they opened again. Despite his forbidding appearance, he gives you a toothy grin.

clawed gloves

These heavy gloves are made out of stiff cloth, and have three steel claws each jutting off of their backs. They are used by mystics to increase their hand to hand damage.

priest spirit

Before you stands the image of a tall, white-robed man, his hair greying with age. His bright green eyes seem to pierce through the insubstantial haze surrounding him, and his commanding personality gives him an almost regal demeanor.

platinum mace

This well-balanced mace has a round head made out of reinforced platinum, with a shaft of black iron. Its head is inscribed with holy symbols, and there is a powerful aura of power surrounding it.

ninja spirit

Before you stands a tall, lean half-orc, wearing full black ninja armour. Twin short swords swing by his side, and his hood is pulled low over his eyes. Despite his unsettling appearance, he manages a short bow at your approach, and motions you closer.

ebony ninjato

This straight singled-edged blade is made out of some strange black metal. Its hilt is forged into the shape of a black pantherm with glowing amber stones for eyes.

flametounge

This long, needle-pointed blade is made of shining high steel. Its hilt is carved obsidian, and is inlayed with small rubies. When drawn, the blade bursts into flame, burning those who it hits.

mage spirit

Before you stands the shadowed image of a black-shrouded dark elf. His bright blue eyes peer from the depths of his hood, and as he looks you up and down, he seems to nod to himself in satisfaction.

obsidian runestaff

This is a six-foot-long staff of a glossy jet black substance. Its surface is heavily carved with swirling runes which seem to pulse and glow redly with an inner light.

bard spirit

The transparent image of a half-elven bard stands before you. She wears bright red silks, and carries a golden harp in her hands.

jewelled main gauche

This long-bladed knife is meant to be wielded in the left hand as a defensive weapon. It has a wide guard and strong blade, which allow it to turn aside most weapons. It is quite ornate, and an eye-sized ruby is set in its pommel.

witchunter spirit

The image of a mammoth, mailed half-ogre floats before you. His huge greatsword hangs from its side as if it were a one-handed blade. At your approach, he looks you up and down with a surprising intelligent gaze.

mithril cutlass

This three foot long blade is crafted out of the finest metal known -- mithril. It blade is so keen it could cut a hair, and its balance is perfect. Its slightly curved blade can slice through steel and flesh with equal ease.

thief spirit

The image of a halfling wavers before you. He wears soft leather clothing, and a shortsword swings by his side. As you approach, he grins and gives a little bow in welcoming.

crystal shortsword

This razor-edged blade is made of chiseled green crystal. Its hilt is crafted out of black steel, and is bound with silver cord.

ranger spirit

Before you stands the image of a leather-clad elf, shrouded in a cloak which makes her nearly invisible. A long blade and ash bow hang from her back. Her long blonde hair is done up into many small braids which cascade down her shoulders, and she looks you over cooly.

witchwood spear

This spear is crafted from a magically-grown hardwood, which when cut from the living tree, can be crafted into high quality magical weapons. This long spear is inlaid with silvery runes, and its foot-long bladed head narrows to a razor-sharp point.

paladin spirit

A shimmering image of a tall man wearing full plate armour stands before you. He rests his hands on the pommel of his great sword, and though you cannot see his face through his visored helm, he seems to exude an aura of quiet sorrow.

shimmering longsword

This exquisite silvery longsword is polished to a mirrorlike sheen. Its hilt is wrapped in cords of gold, and is heavily worked with holy symbols.

missionary spirit

An image of a lithe human woman stands before you. She is clad in dark leather armour, and wields a peculiar curved blade. Her shoulder length red hair flutters like a cloud around her, and her clear grey eyes hold your unwaveringly.

jewelled scimitar

This elaborately worked scimitar is made of the finest high steel, and is worked with many exquisite gems. Its hand guard is delicately worked to portray a snake biting its own tail.

cleric spirit

The image of a short, stocky dwarf clad in shining mail and bearing a massive warhammer stands before you. The dwarf's fierce grin can be seen through his winged helm, and as you approach he raises a mailed fist in greeting.

black flail

This large, jet-black flail has glowing red runes burning along its length. Its three heads are spiked and would cause great damage in battle.

warrior spirit

Before you stands the shimmering figure of a kang warrior, amoured in glittering plate mail. Twin broadswords hang from his back, and as you look him up and down, he bows slightly. With a shock, you recognize him as one of the old heroes of silvermere

golden battleaxe

This heavy, double-bladed hand axe appears to be made of a glittering gold. Its razor-sharp blade gleams in the light, illuminating the miniscule runes covering its surface.

druid spirit

Before you stands the image of a grey-cloaked druid leaning upon a nightblack staff. His piercing grey eyes seem to penetrate your mind, and he looks as if he is evaluating you as the merchants examine livestock.

darkwood staff

This is a living branch from a darkwood tree. It is warm to the touch, and you can feel the lifeblood flowing withing. A silver sheath caps the bottom of the staff, and the centre is carved with intricate looping designs to provide good grip.

gypsy spirit

Before you stands the image of a goblin, dressed in brightly coloured clothes and bearing a broadsword across its back. He grins easily as you approach, and with an elaborate flourish, bows in greeting.

silver rapier

This light sword has a long, thin blade designed for thrusting, which shines with a soft white light. It handles beautifully, and it seems to move with a will of its own.
