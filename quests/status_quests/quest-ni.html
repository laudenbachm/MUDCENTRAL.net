The Ninja Quest is not very difficult (if the regeneration hasn't gotten

outta hand and there are more than 4 monsters in each room). The entrance

is what I call a "Safe Room". Any time you get too low in Hit Points, you

can run there to rest (and the monsters won't follow you in there).

The Owlbear is not difficult, it is about the equivelent of 2 half-ogre

bodyguards. Good luck -Queeg
