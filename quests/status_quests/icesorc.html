The Ice Sorceress

First, travel through the darkwood forest to the path just northeast of the blue tower. Then travel this path to get to the burnt stump:

NE,N,N,NE,NE,NW,N,NW,NW,N,N,N,NW,NW

Then, GO HOLE to get onto the narrow stone passage. Follow this east until you reach the cliff. Here, SAY FAITH and a secret passage will open to the north. Go north into the small chamber. Above you is the rocky cliff. Follow this path to get to the Rocky Trail's fork:

U,S,S,S,SE,S,S,E,E,NE,NE,E,E,NE,E

At this point, follow this path to the frozen caverns:

NE,NE,N,NW,NE,NE,NE,E,E,E

From here, follow the frozen caverns to the Yeti:

NE,E,E,E,E,E,E,E,NE,SE,S,E,SW,E,E,NE,SE,S,S,S

Here you kill the yeti to get the glass key to open a door to the sorceress which you reach later on. Bash the door to the south to enter the Tower. Go 4 rooms south, then bash the gate west. Go west. At this point, TURN CRYSTAL and follow the secret passage NW.

Then go E, Up, W, N, W, NE, E, SE. Here, PULL SWITCH.

Then go W, and PULL ROPE. Go Up, W, S, E. Kill the Ice Golem.
Then use the glass key from the Yeti to open the door above you.

Go Up, W, N, and East to get to the Ice sorceress.

After you fight her, everyone should SIT THRONE in order to get one more AC. This can only be done once, however. If you live, congrats! (hint) Watch out for those ice golems- they can tear anybody apart.

(to get out of the secret passage room on the way back, PUSH WALL.)
(to get out of the secret passage, go all the way west & PUSH BUTTON.)

Dan's Tip: Cast Resist Cold on as many people in you party as possible before entering!

Recommended for parties of level 15-20s. If you are level 15 you can sit throne and get 750k exp, +1 AC.
