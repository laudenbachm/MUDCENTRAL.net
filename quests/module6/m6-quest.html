Module 6 - The Sands of Time Quest
  1. Get a pickaxe, some dwarven ale, waterskins, at least 1250 gold, rope and grapple, someone that can take out traps.
  2. Go to the Nekojin Elder and greet him.
  3. Next, ask elder problems, ask elder saracens, ask elder amulets, ask elder pyramids, ask elder traders.  To see what he says after you ask each thing, click here.
  4. Go to the Scorching Desert to the Saracen Hideout, and kill saracens until you get a saracen head.
  5. Now head on over to the Small Pyramid,to the Seargent. Greet him, ask seargent head,  and he'll grant you entrance to the trader.
  6. Greet the trader, and ask trader seeress, then ask trader favour.. He gives you a box and tell you to go to Meia way back in Silvermere.
  7. Go to Meia, greet her and ask meia box, she gives you a token. It doesn't show in your inventory, but you have one. Now head back to the trader at the Small Pyramid.
  8. Ask trader return, and he lets you go visit the Seeress.
  9. Greet the Seeress, and ask seeress accept. She tells you about a library in the oasis and a book to look at. 
  10. Go to the Shimmering Pool. From the pool, go to the library.
  11. To read the books, you need to type peruse book. Read the blue one first, peruse blue book. Then peruse red book.
  12. Go back to the Seeress and ask seeress return. She says to go find the withered old man, so go to him.
  13. Greet the old man and ask withered dwarven ale. He drinks it and tells you some info, including the secret word Gazmuldduhaz.
  14. Go to the Sandstone Quarry, kill the hideous abomination and mine ore. Bring the ore to Master Martok in Khazarad. Ask master red iron ore and ask master weapon, then he makes you a neato weapon.
  15. Find the Sphinx and answer the riddles (below, if you dare)
  16. Now go to the pharoah and kill him.  You can't touch him unless you use the special weapon.
  17. Now go portal.
  18. Kill the Dao Lord.
  19. Return to Orpheo and ask him Dao Lord and get your reward - the sunstone wrist band.

Riddles

(don't read if you want to figure them out yourself)

These riddles are for the Sphinx.

Riddle #1 - Feed it and it will live. Give it water and it dies.

Answer - Fire

Riddle #2 - I daily view the world around. I never eat yet by my power produce what millions do devour.

Answer - sun

Riddle #3 - At night they come without being fetched. By day they are gone without being stolen.

Answer - stars

Riddle #4 - A father gave his only son half his pile of gold plus one. The wife took half the rest plus two. One gold was left for other use. How much did the father start with?

Answer - fourteen

Read This Too - For this riddle, you don't tell your answer to the Sphinx.  You have yo moves through the rooms and go over the letters to spell it out. If you go over the same letter, or go into a room with the wrong letter, you fall.

Riddle #5 -  The beginning of eternity, the end of time and space.
The beginning of every end and the end of every place.

Answer - time
