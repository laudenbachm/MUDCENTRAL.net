Gloomy Temple
This circular, subterranean chamber is quite large, spanning at least thirty feet across. It is bare for the most part, its smooth stone walls lacking any ornamentation whatsoever. In the centre of the room, a dull iron disc around ten feet in diameter acts as a base for a large, complex sculpture of stone. Strange runes have been etched in the side of the sculpture, and multicoloured crystals imbedded within it pulse with a strange rhythm.
Also here: gaunt one temple master.

The temple master lifts his head at your approach. With the intense, knowledgeable gaze of a Gaunt One ancient, he carefully scans you up and down. In a deep voice that does not echo, he intones:

"Forbidden are these hallowed halls to those not yet mature. State thy business here, fledgeling."

[HP=197/MA=215]:ask gau business
"Only those mature members of the holy race have reason to be here."

"Since you apparently have advanced far enough to stand with other adults of the holy race, you have earned your birthright. Step forward into the Focus."

With a little hesitation, you slowly walk to the centre of the room. The hollow in the centre of the sculpture seems to beckon to you, the glowing crystals pulsing with an urgent light. As you slip into the hollow, you feel a slight tingling wash over you.

Chanting the ancient Song of Seeing, the temple master circles the Focus with an even pace. Crooning his monotonous chant, he begins to weave an intricate spell that seems to be part magic, and part... something else. The strength of his voice rises, as does the power of the pulsing lights.

After a few minutes, the master's voice is reverberating in your skull like thunder, and the light is flashing like lightning. Abruptly, with a brilliant stroke, the light flares out, leaving you dazed!

You stumble out of the Focus, your vision slowly beginning to clear. With a heavy hand, the temple master grips you by the shoulders, speaking softly and saying:

"Go now, as a mature member of our race. One of the chosen of the holy ones. And know that your place in the Circle is assured, when the time comes. Go in peace, and watchfulness."
