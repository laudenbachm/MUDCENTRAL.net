NEUTRAL QUEST 3(level 30 restricted)
Items required to complete this quest are:

Climbing harness (For getting to Kai Master)
Gnomish Fish Helm (For getting the Water Spirit)
Mine Pass (For getting to the Gemstone Juggernaut)
Magma Amulet (For getting to the Efreeti)


Goto Grey Lord
Ask Grey missions

Goto Overgrown Shrine (Located in the Misty Swamp)
Once there type 'Pray'

Goto Gnomish Inventor (In the gnome village)
Ask Inventor Helm

Goto Water Spirit (Located under the frozed caverns)
Ask Spirit Tear

Goto Efreeti (Located in the lava tubes below the lava fields)
Kill the Efreeti
Upon his death get an eternal flame (It just appears in your inventory)

Goto Kai Master (Located above the red stone tunnels above Khaz)
Kill the Kai Master
Upon his death you we get a storm spirit (It just appears in your inventory)

Goto Gemstone Juggernaut (Located in the Dwarven mines east of Khaz)
Kill the Gemstone
Upon his death you will get a Heartstone (it just appears in your inventory)

Goto Overgrown Shrine
Once at the shrine type "Summon Avatar"

Goto Grey Lord
Ask Grey Reward

Quest Complete, you will get a chest to open and 7.5 Million Exp
