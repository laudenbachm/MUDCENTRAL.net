EVIL QUEST 3(level 30 restricted)
Goto Balthazar
Ask Bal work

Goto Shifty Dwarf
Greet Shifty
Ask him everything highlighted.

Goto Kobold King in the caves on the NE area of Misty Valley
Kill the king
Take the iron crown the king drops

Goto Shifty Dwarf in the Silvermere sewers
Greet Shifty
Ask Shifty crown
Open pouch (you should now have a signet ring)

Goto Duergar Lord deep in the dwarven mines
Ask Lord Balthazar

Goto Two-Headed Troll in the Misty Swamp
Kill the troll (you will see a message where you grab some retchweed upon his death)

Goto Mushroom Caves on the east side of the second level of the Black Caves
Find the room with a patch of black mushrooms in it
Harvest mushrooms

Goto Duergar Lord
Ask Lord indredients

Goto Huge Obsidian Golem in the dwarven mines
Kill the golem
Take the obsidian talisman he drops

Goto Duergar Lord
Ask Lord transport
***Everyone in your actual party will be transported, alignments do not matter

Kill Champion Gudruk
Push button
Go one room into the kitchen
Poison food

Goto Duergar Lord
Ask Lord return

Goto Balthazar
Ask Bal alliance

Quest Complete, you will get a chest to open and 7.5 Million Exp
