GOOD QUEST 1(lvl 10 restricted)

Goto wounded messenger in the Temple Halls
Greet wounded messenger
Ask messenger mission

Goto Guildmaster
Greet guildmaster
Ask guildmaster strangers
Ask guildmaster wounded messenger

Goto wounded messenger
Ask wounded messenger guildmaster
Ask wounded messenger accept
(He gives you a letter ((not in your inv)) to deliver to Annora)

Goto Chancellor Annora
Ask Annora letter
(She gives you a letter ((not in your inv)) to deliver to Commander Markus)

Goto Commander Markus
Greet Commander Markus
Ask Markus letter
(He gives you a box ((not in your inv)) to take back to Annora)

Goto Chancellor Annora
Ask Annora return

You have completed the 1st good quest, you will get 150K exp and a white gold quest ring!
