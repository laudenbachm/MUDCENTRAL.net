S neaking Races

Races: Dark-elf, Elf, Halfling, Goblin, Nekojin

Pro's Vs Con's: Call yourself crazy , a sneaking paladin only if you want a really huge exp table but it can be done if you are a dedicated player. and stealth has its advantages in everything espicially when you can have the ac as well. Like the warriors however you won't sneak well till at least lvl 30. Try a dark-elf, nekojin or an elf for extra mana and with a paladin you'll need all you can get.

N on Sneaking Races

Races: Human, Dwarf, Gnome, Half-elf, Half-Orc, Half-Ogre, Kang, and Guant

Pro's Vs Con's:Now here is a tank they have spells to back up their hps and the AC as well. Who needs to sneak when you can walk in and swat dragons like flies. The most obvious race would be a dwarf, perfect for their high wisdom, but also for the extra enc. You may even get crazy and try to make a half-elf for extra mana. Rick Fanslow suggests the following, "OK here is the Thing.. you MUST choose at level 0 how your gonna make your pallie.. your either gonna make a warrior or a priest outta him till around 25th level. The only decent race to choose is dwarf the extra encumberance is worth everything else." Kang would be a decent second choice.
