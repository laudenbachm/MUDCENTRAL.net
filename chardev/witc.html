Making a Sneaker
Stat Suggestions (In order of raise)

  • Health = get this to 70 first
  • Agility = raise it 10-20, should be easy to to adds little stealth and dodge
  • Strength = get to 80, hardest stat of sneakers to raise besides health but most important, improves stealth and bs, besides being able to carry more armour
  • Intel = raise to 50, improves stealth, and crits
  • Charm = raise to 50, improves stealth, crits and dodge
  • Health = max this next, get lots of hps, Mwhahaha!!!
  • Agility = raise to 90 or so, swing more and dodge more, also improve stealth tremendously
  • Strength = max it, hit harder and carry more armour
  • Intel = raise to 100 or max, makes you start crit-ing everwhere and improves stealth
  • Agility = max this
  • Charm = raise to 90, improves stealth, and you'll notice you'll get hit much less
  • Wisdom = raise this only after every stat is at least 80

Making a Tank (non sneaking)
Stat Suggestions (In order of raise)

  • Health to 80 to start, as well as Strength as high as you can get it with your remaining cp. Hitpoints and accuracy are your friend.
  • Agility to at least 50.
  • Strength to 90 for combat effectiveness, and the ability to carry better armor.
  • Agility to at least 65, so you can get more swings
  • Wisdom, Charm and Intellect to at least 50 so you aren�t penalized for having any under strength stats. This should all be possible by around 17-20th level, depending on race.
  • Now raise Health to at least 100, you�ll start resting more effectively, and you�ll start actually needing those hitpoints.
  • Strength can now go to racial maximum � you�ll need to hit harder and more accurately to take on tougher monsters.
  • Agility should go to at least 80 now, as it has a strong impact on your swings, and some impact on accuracy. Smash will probably start to get stale for anything but a Half-Ogre around now. Consider this preparation to start swinging.
  • Health can now go to whatever your racial maximum is. You�ll notice much higher average hitpoint rolls once your health is at maximum.
  • Agility should now go to racial maximum.
  • Charm should go to racial max next.
  • Wisdom and Intellect can be raised as you wish now. Intellect has a dubious effect on crits and a definite effect on perception. Wisdom is the determining stat of your magic resistance. Some players may choose to raise Wisdom earlier.
