S neaking Races

Races: Dark-elf, Elf, Halfling, Goblin, Nekojin

Pro's Vs Con's: 3 words, sneak, backstab, dfir. Granted they have a slighly higher exp table, but the stealth is worth it. They start out with not very much strength all around or health, but agility and intel are some of the best starting stats for this group.

Making a Sneaker
Stat Suggestions (In order of raise)

  • Health = 65-80 Get the health bonus more hp the merrier
  • Agility = 70 get dodge, swings and stealth
  • Strength = 60 just enough to carry your current armour
  • Intel = 60-70 helps greatly with spell casting
  • Charm = 50 increases stealth and just about evrything
  • Health = 95 get your last hp bonus
  • Agility = 90 helps with fighting stealth and dodge
  • Intel = 90 helps with your spellcasting
  • Charm = 60 increases stealth and just about evrything
  • Wisdom = 50 helps with spell casting a little bit
  • Agility = 100 helps with fighting stealth and ac
  • Intel = 100 helps with your spellcasting
  • Strength = 80 so you can weild the sunsword and to cary more and fight better
  • Charm = 80 increases stealth and spellcasting
  • Wisdom = 60-70 increasing spellcasting
  • Charm = 100 max out your stealth and helps with a little of everything
  • Strength = 100 hit hard and carry more
  • Wisdom = 80-90 helps with spellcasting
  • Health = 100 rest bonus, should be around lvl 47
  • Raise whatever stat isn't 100
