S neaking Races

Races: Goblin, Dark-elf, Elf,

Pro's Vs Con's: Sneaking priests , they sneak and they cast what else could you want :) they are a worthwhile combination if your not looking for a ton of hps.

Making a Sneaker
Stat Suggestions (In order of raise)

  • Health = get this to 80 first
  • Agility = raise it 10-20, should be easy to to adds little stealth and dodge
  • Strength = get to 60, just enough to carry your armour at light, hardest stat of sneakers to raise besides health, improves stealth and bs, besides being able to carry more armour
  • Willpower = 80 Helps a priestly spell casting tremendously
  • Intel = raise to 50, improves stealth
  • Charm = raise to 50, improves stealth and dodge
  • Health = get this to MAX next, get lots of hps, Mwhahaha!!!
  • Wisdom = MAX for better spellcasting and Mres
  • Agility = raise to MAX, swing more and dodge more, also improve stealth tremendously
  • Intel = raise to 80, makes you start crit-ing everwhere and improves stealth
  • Charm = raise to 80 or less, improves stealth, and you'll notice you'll get hit much less
  • Intel = raise to max helps spell casting as well as mana regen and critting
  • Charm = raise to max help everything espicially stealth and dodge
  • Strength = only keep enough to carry your equipment but raise this all the way up if it is your last stat to raise its all you have left :)
