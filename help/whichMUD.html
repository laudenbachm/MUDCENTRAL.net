MUDLive

No afk (away from keys) scripting is allowed in this game. Players vary considerably in range. This is a very newbie friendly game, but can also be a hotbed of PVP fighting at times. Most limited items are taken, but people tend to share a lot. PVP fighting is restricted to a 10 level range.

Clubmet MUD

One of the oldest versions around. This game has been running since the original beta test of the first version of MajorMUD to hit the market. Many players are high level, but newbie help is available. Limited items are mostly taken, but people share well. Away from keys scripting is allowed. This game has unlimited PVP.

SanctuaryMUD

This game has PVP combat completely disabled. If you like to play without being bothered by other players, this is the game for you. Most players are high level, but friendly. Most limited items are taken, but people tend to share. Away from keys scripting is allowed.

GarbageDump MUD

This game is for adults only. You must provide proof of age and be 18+ to enter this game. Pvp and afk scripting are both allowed in this game.

Masterpiece MUD

Originally based out of Seattle, this game has also been running since the original beta test of MajorMUD. Most players are high level, but some limited items are available. This is a friendly realm, with PVP combat restricted to a 3 level range and rare. Away from keyboard scripting is allowed.

SanctuaryMUD2

A newer version of Sanctuary. Having opened as a new realm in August of 1999, most players are of a mid to lower level. Many monsters have not been killed yet, and some limited items are still up for grabs. Away from keys scripting is allowed.

CouncilMUD

This is a half & half game. You may play afk from 12am to 7pm Central Time Zone. However, from 7pm to 12am, you must stay live and at keys. This helps us to keep it a social game. Many bosses have not been killed and limited items are available.

SuperMUD

This is our contest realm. The game is reset at regular intervals and has a themed contest each time with fun prizes.
