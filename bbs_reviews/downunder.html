Downunder BBS reviewed by "Nity" - January 2003
RATING :
Telnet - telnet://downunderbbs.com
Webpage - http://www.downunderbbs.com

My two weeks stay at Downunder BBS was very pleasant. The board is located in Australia, but I had very few problems with any lag during my time there. The SysOp and the players are all very friendly. The SysOp power leveled me and gave me plenty of cash and was extremely helpful. The realm is quiet off and on and has its peak times when it's full of chitter chatter. There are a lot of limiteds up for grabs, hoarding is watched very closely. This board has a pvp level of 5, but there is a non-pvp gang which cannot be attacked under any circumstances. It caters to both pvpers and non-pvpers. There was an average of 20-25 characters online during my stay, as well as a bot that the sysop has setup to allow players to check the status of bosses and add evil points to themselves. If you're looking for a friendly group of players with a helpful sysop, this is a great board to check out! I gave this board a 9 out of 10 because it could use a few more players to chatter in gossips and there is always a tad of lag telnetting from the US to another country.
