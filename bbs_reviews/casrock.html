CasRock reviewed by "Nity" - November 2003
RATING :
Casrock BBS
Telnet: casrock.com

I had the pleasure of making a visit to Casrock BBS. This is a very small and quiet non-pvp realm. There was an average of 6-8 characters online during my stay there (1 dupe per person). The mudop, Cyrus, was more than helpful with money, items and experience. This BBS offers sys goto and sys map to all players at no additional cost. Being such a small realm, many many of the items are still available, there are a large amount of limiteds just waiting to be claimed by players. The top 10 is very small, ranging from 50bil down to a mere 9mil exp. The few problems I found during my stay were spurts of lag, (not enough to kill me) very quiet gossips, and the BBS only has a 16 user license for mud. Should the 16 lines fill, dupes will be gotten rid of and it will become 1 account per person. The SysOp is very active, logging into the mud at least 3 times during my stay to check on players and the realm. He was very easy to get ahold of through e-mail as well, always responding very quickly.

I scored this BBS an 8 out of 10 because of the lag and the very quiet atmosphere. With some new and talkative players, this could become a very thriving realm in no time. I recommend this board to anyone looking for a friendly, quiet environment where limiteds are never a problem.
